3. ctable()

This section is to calculate smoker and diseased cross-table. ctable() produces cross-tabulations between two factors or any discrete data, with total, rows or columns proportions, as well as marginal totals.

out<-ctable(tobacco$smoker, tobacco$diseased, prop = "r",chisq = TRUE)
print(out, method = "render")
